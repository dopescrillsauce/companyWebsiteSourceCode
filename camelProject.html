Camel (RJR)
camel.com
As an Experience (UX) Architect working at an Advertising Agency, I was client facing with RJ Reynolds executives and the agencies digital team to guide UX design & strategy for Camel's quarterly promotions on their website. In addition, I also helped out to kick off a website redesign initiative.
Who were we designing for?
Camel Tobacco Consumers (ATC's) : The users we were designing for are referred to as "American Tobacco Consumers" (ATC's). The design and user experience was driven by what the marketing team referred to as "hip" and "artistic" tobacco consumers.

Approach & Design
Creative Strategy : Inspire ATCs to show their vision through mobile photography with prizes that spark originality and self-expression.

Elevator Pitch : Showcasing some of the best new mobile photographers’ styles on our pack design, the Camel In Focus Project invites ATCs to share their own view of the world through contests, custom photo ﬁlters and prizeﬁlled challenges.May the most original and creative shots win.

Design Challenges : All designs had to account for the tobacco industries legal restrictions and considerations. This gave our team "tight and strict" parameters during the creative and implementation phases.

Results : Shipped & launched a Q4 website promotion where ATC's engaged with the Camel brand by submitting photographs that were on brand. By the end of the promotion all the marketing team benchmarks were met.
