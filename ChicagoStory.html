THE CHICAGO STORY

It was a chilly fall day in October 2009...

...and a guy received a phone call from his best friend.

The guy's best friend had just moved to Chicago...

...and was saying how awesome it was.
Then the friend asked...

"Why don't you just move to the windy city too?!"

3 days later...

The guy packed a bag and was off to ChiTown.

He started networking & job hunting right away.

...and couch surfing definitly happened.

The guy worked odd jobs for a couple months...

...and worked at night on techie things.

Until one day...

...the guy shared a taxi with a stranger.

That stranger was looking for a UX Designer.

A week later that guy had his first UX gig.

He hasn't left his passion for working in tech.

All you have to do is trust me.
