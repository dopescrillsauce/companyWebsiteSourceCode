Chairlift Software
chairlift.io
During my time as the Design Director for a software start-up, I met with team members at various roles on a daily basis to discuss the design and build of a Human Resource Performance Management product suite. The products goal is to serve as a performance management solution that "Elevates Engagement" of employees within organizations.
Design Process Overview (PDF)
Who is Chairlift for?
HR Professionals (Admin) : People within an organization who need a comprehensive platform to help track and keep up with HR performance management tasks.

Executives : Business leaders who want to be able to see high level overviews of HR related data and information.

People Managers : The employees who have frequent and personal conversations and meetings with direct reports.

Employee (default user) : Employees who need to input HR data into the system at a basic level.
Challenges & Solutions
Primary Challenge : Many HR performance management processes are time consuming and non-HR professionals either do not have time or do not care about Human Resource Management. How do we design for this?

Proposed Solution (high level) : Design and build user experiences that are "uninterrupted" experiences & are integrated into employees routines but still allow them to be engaged and provide useful HR data to the organization.
Product Features
360 Feedback : Empowers managers and employees to collect real-time 360 degree feedback anytime anywhere.

Performance Reviews : Customizable review forms to fit organization’s needs and workflows.

Employee Recognition : Foster a culture of recognition in an engaging way that increases productivity and improves morale.

OKRs ( Objectives & Key Results ) : Inspire your team to move together in the right direction by connecting your employee, team, and organization’s objectives with measurable results.

Employee Directory : A secure mobile friendly employee directory which automatically creates and updates organizational charts.

Talent Analytics : Gain meaningful insights and visualize how your employee’s performance impacts your organizational goals.

Automated HR Systems Integration : Directly synchronize your data across a wide variety of HRIS applications including ADP, UltiPro, and BambooHR.

1:1 Coaching : Documented one-on-one meetings that coach employees and help them develop professionally.
Technology & Frameworks
Web : HTML / CSS / React JS
iOS : Objective-C / Swift
Android : Java / Angular JS
