So what's my story?
I moved to Chicago in the fall of 2012 as an Urban Designer.
Of course I "roughed it" for a little bit to be young, wild & free.
During the evenings I satisfied my curiousities for technology & how we design with it.
Until one day...
I shared a cab ride with a stranger.
They were looking for an entry level UX Designer to join their digital team.
One week later I had my first gig as a UX Designer!
The rest is history.
So let's enjoy the process...
...no matter how "big" or "small" the project.
Are you ready to build amazing digital experiences?
I know I am.

Travelling around this amazing planet is truely a lot of fun.
